ABOUT

A digital product designer, UC Berkeley dropout, Millenial, startup consultant, and serial entrepreneur, Alison has had the advantage of being involved with the good and bad of many new startup ventures over the years.


...Their failures, their successes, their equity structures, their financing, their spending, their products, their hiring strategies, and their operational and leadership issues.


Currently, Alison is a full time serial founder and consultant with operations in New York, Silicon Valley, and Chicago. She is working on a book about the web design industry, growing her consulting agency, and developing a couple of startups with the help of her teams and lots of apps.
