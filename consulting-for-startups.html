ABOUT:

A digital product designer, UC Berkeley dropout, Millenial, startup consultant, and serial entrepreneur, Alison has had the advantage of seeing the ins and outs of many new startup ventures over the years.


...Their failures, their successes, their equity structures, their financing, their spending, their products, their hiring strategies, and their operational and leadership issues.


Currently, Alison is a full time serial founder and consultant with operations in New York, Silicon Valley, and Chicago.
